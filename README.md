# Anime.sec

Anime.sec is a website dedicated to anime fans, offering a curated hub for discovering, tracking, and discussing anime series and films.

## About

Anime.sec brings together everything an anime fan needs in one place — from trending series to hidden gems, community discussions to episode guides.

## Features

- **Browse Catalog** — Explore a growing library of anime titles across genres like action, romance, fantasy, slice of life, and more.
- **Search & Filter** — Quickly find shows by genre, year, studio, rating, or popularity.
- **Watchlist** — Keep track of what you're currently watching, plan to watch, or have completed.
- **Ratings & Reviews** — Rate shows and read what other fans think.
- **Episode Guides** — Get synopses and details for individual episodes without spoilers.
- **Recommendations** — Discover new anime based on your viewing history and preferences.
- **Community Discussions** — Join threads and forums to talk theories, favorite characters, and upcoming releases.

## Getting Started

1. Create an account or browse as a guest.
2. Search for an anime title or explore by genre.
3. Add shows to your watchlist to keep track of progress.
4. Rate and review titles you've finished.
5. Join the community to discuss with other fans.

## Who It's For

Whether you're a casual viewer looking for your next binge or a longtime otaku who wants a place to organize your anime journey, Anime.sec aims to be a friendly, easy-to-navigate space for all things anime.
